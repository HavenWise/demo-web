ⓘ How do we estimate your savings?

Havenwise savings estimates consider three factors:

  • Efficiency: Samsung heat pumps operated by Havenwise typically run 20% more efficiently than they would on weather compensation (see our Blog post).
  • Load shifting: We ran your heat pump at cheaper times of day to align with your Octopus Intelligent Go tariff, then compared the energy cost with your average energy price.
  • Seasonal changes: Hot water costs are similar year round, but we look at monthly average outdoor temperatures to estimate your heating costs in colder months.
